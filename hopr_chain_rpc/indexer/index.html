Extends the RpcOperations type with functionality needed by the Indexer component.

The functionality required functionality is defined in the HoprIndexerRpcOperations trait, which is implemented for RpcOperations hereof. The primary goal is to provide a stream of BlockWithLogs filtered by the given LogFilter as the new matching blocks are mined in the underlying blockchain. The stream also allows to collect historical blockchain data.

For details on the Indexer see the chain-indexer crate.
